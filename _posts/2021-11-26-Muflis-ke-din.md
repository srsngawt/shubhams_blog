# Muflis ke Din

Muflis ke din beet jaane do?

Fir to gadha bhi chad niklega.

Khuda teri niyati ne mujhe Benawa kar diya,

Tu Dekh, Meri Taqat, Mujhe Badshahi dilayegi.

*~ Shubham Sangawat*

*`Muflis`* *`Poetry`* *`Self-Confidence`*
